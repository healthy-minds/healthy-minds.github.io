Welcome to MeCare

We don't view mental health as a money-making opportunity or answer to corporate interests. We are proud to be recognized as a transparent and trustworthy nonprofit provider of quality health information.

We offer you the guidance and encouragement you need to find hope, take charge of your mental health, and feel better. We focus on what you need to know to understand the challenges you face, make informed decisions and take positive steps.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower people with the information and insight they need to improve their mental health and make healthy changes. We focus on what you need to know to understand the challenges you face, make informed decisions and take positive steps. We do this by providing a safe place to share stories and find support for others who are dealing with mental health disorders

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide holistic and wellness focused services.
These will help promote social-emotional development, prevent development of mental health challenges and address social-emotional problems that currently exist by using evidence-based strategies in our services and providing staff training and health consultation to everyone.
